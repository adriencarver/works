Emo

It's 2088 and Elizabeth London is turning 100 today. Time to go to the Epicity to get retired aka medically euthanized. She’s aided by her Emotionally-Minded Omnibot, an AI hospice nurse. On the way down, she listens to an old mix CD-- a parting gift from her first boyfriend in the fall of 2006.

The songs activate memories long forgotten, and the story alternates between Elizabeth reliving moments of her first week at college and her journey through the dystopian underground civilization humanity has built for itself in the wake of unchecked climate change.
